Deviled Eggs

My grandmother taught me this recipe when I was younger.

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sweet pickle relish
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne pepper

Method

  1. Get a pot and place the eggs in the pot.
  2. Fill the pot with water.
  3. Boil the eggs for an hour.
  4. Pour the hot water off of the eggs and pour cold water on them. (This makes them easier to peel.)
  5. Peel all of the eggs.
  6. Slice each egg in half and place the yolk in a bowl.
  7. Lay the egg whites to the side.
  8. Grab the bowl with the egg yolks and smash them with a fork or spoon.
  9. Use a large spoon to add two scoops of mayonnaise to the yolks.
  10. Add one large spoon full of sweet pickle relish.
  11. Add a dash of salt and pepper.
  12. Add a spoon full of sugar.
  13. Stir mixture together.
  14. If the consistancy of the mixture seems too think add a little more mayonnaise.(You don't want it to be too runny.)
  15. Taste a little bit of the mixture and see if there is anything that you need to adjust the taste of.
  16. Once you have the mixture ready, grab a spoon and spoon on the mixture in the egg whites.
  17. After you finish, take the cayenne pepper and sprikle over the eggs.
